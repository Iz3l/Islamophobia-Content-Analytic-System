Understanding Islamophobia

Islamophobia manifests as fear, prejudice, and hatred towards Muslims and Islam, often expressed through discrimination, stereotyping, and hate speech across various platforms.

Platform-Specific Manifestations:

  • Reddit: Subreddit discussions, comment threads
  • X (Twitter): Tweets, replies, hashtags
  • YouTube: Video comments, video content descriptions
