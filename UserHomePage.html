What is Islamophobia?

Islamophobia refers to fear, prejudice, and hatred towards Muslims and the Islamic religion, leading to discrimination, exclusion, and violence.

Common Examples:

  • Stereotyping all Muslims as terrorists or extremists
  • Discrimination in employment, education, or housing
  • Verbal harassment and hate speech online
  • Physical attacks on mosques or individuals
  • Media portrayal linking Islam with violence
